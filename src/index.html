Cat of the day

The Tokinese Cat Tonkinese is a domestic cat breed produced by crossbreeding between
the Siamese and Burmese. Members of the breed share many of their
parents' distinctively lively, playful personality traits and are
similarly distinguished by a pointed coat pattern in a variety of colours.

Colour point variations of Tonkinese cats Tonkinese are currently officially recognized by the Cat Fanciers'
Association (CFA) in four base colors: natural (a medium brown),
champagne (a paler buff-beige), blue, and platinum. Some European
associations also accept red, cream, caramel, apricot and tortoiseshell,
cinnamon and fawn.
